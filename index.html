Esso, the live lecture feedback tool

6.813: User Interface Design and Implementation

Cambridge, MA | Spring 2017

Hunter Gatewood, Rachel Simpson

Our project aims to address the drawbacks of the in-person lecture by making it easier for lecturers to determine when students are confused or distracted, as well as interpret feedback more effectively. For students, we wish to lower the activation energy required to provide feedback or ask questions.
